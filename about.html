Our Story

A passion for meat, a love for Nairobi

The Meatlovers Journey

Founded in 2010, Meatlovers Restaurant began with a simple vision: to bring Nairobi the finest grilled meat experience imaginable. What started as a small family-run eatery with just 10 seats has grown into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations.

Our founder, Chef David Omondi, grew up in the countryside of Kenya, where he learned the art of traditional meat grilling from his grandmother. Those childhood memories of gathering around open fires, the aroma of sizzling meat, and the joy of sharing meals with loved ones became the inspiration for Meatlovers.

Today, we combine those traditional techniques with modern culinary innovation. We source our meats exclusively from local Kenyan farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Every cut is carefully selected, expertly seasoned, and grilled to perfection.

But Meatlovers is more than just a restaurant ‚Äì it's a gathering place. It's where families celebrate, friends reconnect, and memories are made over exceptional food. Our team is dedicated to creating an atmosphere that's warm, welcoming, and authentically Kenyan.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the Meatlovers story.

2010

The Beginning

Meatlovers opens its doors with just 10 seats and a simple charcoal grill. Word quickly spreads about "the best nyama choma in town."

2013

First Expansion

Due to overwhelming demand, we expand to a larger location with 80 seats and introduce our signature T-Bone steak.

2016

Award Winner

Meatlovers wins "Best Restaurant in Nairobi" at the Kenya Food Awards, recognizing our commitment to quality.

2019

Kitchen Renovation

We invest in state-of-the-art grilling equipment while preserving our traditional techniques, allowing us to serve even more customers.
